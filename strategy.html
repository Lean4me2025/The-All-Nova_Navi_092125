Turn Your Purpose Into a Plan

Use the Five W's + How to focus your job search: Who, What, Why, When, Where — and How you'll execute.

Who — Who are you becoming?

Root your choices in your Nova traits. These traits guide which roles will feel fulfilling.

What — What role fits?

Seek roles that map to your traits. Fit first, then check salary. Navi recommends roles and shows why they match.

Why — Why this path?

Clarify your motivation: growth, impact, income, balance. Your 'why' keeps you steady through the process.

When — Timing & expectations

Market reality: ~10–20 applications → 1 interview on average, and roughly 1 in 5 interviews → offer. Don't take early silence personally — it's the math.

Where — Location & mobility

Decide whether remote, local, or relocation fits your purpose. Navi's Job Tools show geographic concentrations for specific roles.

How — Execution

  • Use Resume Tools to align your profile.
  • Use Job Intel to target companies.
  • Use Training Hub to close gaps.
  • Track progress in the Dashboard.

Reality Check & Encouragement

Applying doesn't mean failure. Use Navi's tools to increase quality of applications — persistence + focus wins.
